Procedure

1) Connected CRO ch1 at clock (Wk) socket & observed it.
2) Connected CRO ch2 at the bit clock (Bk) socket & observed it.
3) Connected CRO ch1 at RF carrier socket & observed the waveform.
4) Connected CRO ch1 NRZ data socket & observed it.
5) Pushed on start switch & observed PN signal.
6) Observed spreading signal.
7) Observed DS-SS signal with reference the NRZ data & spreading code signal.
8) Observed recovered spreading code at o/p of DS-SS modulator.
9) Observed the filter spreading code at o/p of LPF. Keep noise level at minimum.
10) Observed recovered pure NRZ data at o/p of comparator.
