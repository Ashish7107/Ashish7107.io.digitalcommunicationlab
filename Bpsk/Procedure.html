Procedure

1. O/P of pattern Gen is connected to I/P of ‘OR’ Gate.
2. O/P of OR gate is connected to I/P of transmitter i.e. (i/p of MULT. Block).
3. O/P of ‘MULT’ Block i.e BPSK O/P is connected to I/P of 1496 Sq.cct.
4. O/P of 1496 Sq.cct is connected to I/P of Band Pass Filter.
5. O/P of Band Pass fitter is connected to I/P of %2 N/W.
6. O/P of %2 N/W is connected to I/P of phase comparator.
7. BPSK O/P is connected to I/P 2 of phase comparator.
8.Observed signal at different test points together with I/P bit pattern.
9. filter O/P & COMP. Block O/P. The O/P of COMP. Block is required detected O/P.
